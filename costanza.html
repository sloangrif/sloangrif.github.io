Simple Shapes

The goal of this assignment was to create a graphical representation of an image using simple shapes. I decided to replicate a comical figure in pop culture, George Costanza. I decided on a cooler tones to let the iconic Seinfeld logo stand out. I incorporated a George Costanza quote to make the piece represent the true nature of the character. Chose a font that resembles that of the basic type in a screenplay. This was to give the idea that this is a character description.
