When No one Is Looking

What happens when no one is looking? This is an animation short I created putting a new spin on the saying 'Dance like nobodys watching'. Here I modeled my representation of Baymax from Disney's Big Hero 6, and animated him to dance when he realizes everyone has left the room. I modeled, animated, lighted, textured and cut this animated short into a video.
