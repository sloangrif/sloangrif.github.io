Mapn

I designed for a local startup company here in Gainesville named, Mapn™. These are mockups I made for the three main pages of the web app. Mapn™ is a video broadcasting app focused on small towns and businesses. The designs shown here include the main map page, which acts as the center of functionality for the app, a business pin page, as well as a business analytics page. While designing, I strived to keep the design as simple as possible, while still maintaining an interactive experience for the user.
