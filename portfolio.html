ToDo App

Apart of the ending of my internship at Grooveshark, I was given the task of designing a ToDo App. I began by documenting everything that needed to be include inside of the page. This include the types of pages, functionality, and what I would allow the user to customize. This process include research of multiple other ToDo Apps inside of the App store. Once I was able to determine the most important elements to be included in the app, I began to sketch out designs and eventually create the app on Photoshop. There were a few different interations but the final product is shown here. I have included the three main pages of the app here. This includes the Landing Page, Detail Page, as well as what it looks like to use some of the apps functionality.
