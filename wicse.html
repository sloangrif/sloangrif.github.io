UF WiCSE

UF Women in Computer Science and Engineering is an the University of Florida’s ACM-W student chapter. It is an organization that strives to get more women involved in computer science and engineering fields. I am the webmaster for this organization and I was lucky enough to have the ability to completely redesign and develop our website. My goal was to create a layout that would look welcoming and easy for anyone to understand and navigate. The website doesn’t use any templates, it is all designed and coded by myself to create the right atmosphere and features we want for our organizations website. It is continually updated and tweaked to remain update and add new features that we think will make our web presence more user friendly and instructional for members to use.
