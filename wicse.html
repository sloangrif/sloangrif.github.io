UF WiCSE

UF Women in Computer Science and Engineering is the University of Florida’s ACM-W student chapter. It is an organization that strives to get more women involved in computer science and engineering fields. I serve as the webmaster for this organization and, recently, had the privilege of completely redesigning and developing our website. My goal was to create a clear and welcoming layout for anyone to navigate easily. The website does not use any templates; instead, I designed and coded it all myself to fit our organization’s atmosphere. It is continually updated and tweaked to ensure our features and events remain up-to-date, making it continuously more user-friendly and instructional for our members to use.
